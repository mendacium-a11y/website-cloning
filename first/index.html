BOOTSTRAP INCUBATION AND ADVISORY FOUNDATION

Uniting and Empowering the Startup Ecosystem

THE STARTUP ECOSYSTEM, BROUGHT TOGETHER

Bootstrap Incubation and Advisory Foundation exists with the core purpose of enabling a sense of community – to bring together early-stage startup founders, investors, business enthusiasts, industry experts and other stakeholders of the startup ecosystem, under one roof.

We believe in the power of collaboration; our aim is to mitigate the barriers that exist between the stakeholders of the startup space and build a powerful startup infrastructure in India.
